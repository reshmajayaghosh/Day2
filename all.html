ALL DISTRICTS



Kerala has 14 separate districts, each famous for its own set of unique destinations and features. Following is a list of that can be used to gain information on each individual district based on your preference. This is to aid you in the process of gaining more data regarding the areas you would like to visit and plan your trip accordingly.



Alappuzha

Alappuzha is one of the 14 districts in the state of Kerala in India. It was formed as Alleppey District on August 17, 1957. The name of the district was officially changed to Alappuzha in 1990.

Kannur

Kannur District is one of the 14 districts along the west coast in the state of Kerala, India. The town of Kannur is the district headquarters and gives the district its name. The old name, Cannanore is the anglicised form of the Malayalam name "Kannur". Kannur district is bounded by Kasaragod District to the north.

Kasaragod

Wayanad

Wayanad District is a district in the northeast of Kerala state, India with headquarters at the town of Kalpetta. The district was formed on 1 November 1980 as the 12th district in Kerala by carving out areas from Kozhikode and Kannur districts.
